Password Strength Checker

Type a password to see how it scores against five guidance rules.

  • At least 8 characters Not met
  • A lowercase letter Not met
  • An uppercase letter Not met
  • A digit Not met
  • A symbol Not met

These rules are guidance, not gatekeepers. Length matters most — longer is always better; avoid common words and predictable patterns. Meeting all five rules at a sufficient length (at least 12 characters) earns a Strong rating, but no checker can promise a password is unguessable.
